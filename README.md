# Quintiles Transnational Holdings

Quintiles Transnational Holdings was a Fortune 500 contract research organization (CRO) headquartered in Durham, North Carolina, that provided biopharmaceutical development and commercial outsourcing services to the pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and medical device industries. Founded in 1982 by Dennis Gillings, Quintiles became one of the largest CROs in the world and was at the center of late-stage drug development for many of the top-selling medicines of the 2000s and 2010s.

In October 2016, Quintiles merged with IMS Health to form **IQVIA**, a global leader in advanced analytics, technology solutions, and contract research services for the life sciences industry. As a result, the legacy Quintiles Transnational Holdings entity no longer operates as an independent company, and any developer-facing assets, public APIs, or partner integrations have been transferred to and consolidated under the IQVIA brand.

## API Status

No public APIs are currently published under the Quintiles Transnational Holdings name. Developer resources, data products, and clinical research APIs are now offered through IQVIA. There is no OpenAPI specification associated with this repository.
